Explore my 3D design section, a showcase of my crafted creations. The pieces display a fusion of technical skills and artistic visions, showcasing my ideas on a canvas.

Bounce

Welcome to "Bounce", my very first game, crafted using the Tkinter library in Python. "Bounce" presents a classic arcade experience where players take command of a paddle, using it to keep a bouncing ball within the bounds of the screen. As introductory project into game development, "Bounce" introduces the fundamentals of interactive programming while providing an enjoyable gaming experience with levels and difficulties.
